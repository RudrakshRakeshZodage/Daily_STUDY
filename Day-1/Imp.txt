Question :-- Maximum and minimum of an array using minimum number of comparisons


1. By Sorting the array - O(n log n) Time and O(1) Space

2. Iterating the array - O(n) Time and O(1) Space

3. Dividing array in two parts - O(n) Time and O(log n) Space

4. Comparing in pairs - O(n) Time and O(1) Space
